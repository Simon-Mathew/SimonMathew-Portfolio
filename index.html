About

Background

I'm a recent Information Technology graduate from Torrens University, and I recently completed a software engineering internship at Brisbane Capital, where I shipped reusable Angular components, SEO integrations, and worked inside a Docker-based CI/CD pipeline alongside the team.

I taught myself to build by building. Most of what I know comes from shipping real projects end to end and reading a lot of source. Lately I've been picking up React to round out my Angular background, and I'm interested in where AI tooling fits into everyday apps — one of my projects uses a vision model to read data straight off an image.

I'm based in Queensland and open to relocating to Melbourne or Sydney for the right team.
